About us

Whisker Haven Cat Shelter

Whisker Haven Cat Shelter is a sanctuary dedicated to providing love, care, and a forever home for cats in need. Our mission is to rescue abandoned, stray, and surrendered cats, giving them a second chance at a happy life.
